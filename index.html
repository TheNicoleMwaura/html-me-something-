Kenya

The most revered of Africa’s safari destinations, Kenya is located in East Africa and boasts the Masai Mara National Park, renowned for its abundant wildlife and annual Great Migration.

It is a country brimming with romance, thrill and adventure nothing says “classic safari” quite like a Kenyan safari.

Discover Kenya's iconic experiences

1. The Great Migration

The great Serengeti wildebeest migration is a wildlife spectacle with over 2 million migratory animals moving clockwise through the Serengeti and Masai Mara Reserve in kenya

The migration is fundamental to their survival, consuming over 4000 tons per day these animals need to move & feed mainly they move to greener pastures

2. Mount kenya

The largest mountain in Kenya is also the second highest in Africa

With its rugged snowy peaks and glaciers along the upper slopes, Mt Kenya is one of the most beautiful places for hicking and climbing

3. Flocks of Flamingos

Lake Nakuru National Park is home to a vast number of bird species, most notably huge flocks of flamingos.

They have been known to number in their millions, though the lake has seen a decrease in recent times due to pollution.

3. Lamu Island

Lamu town was founded around 1370 as a Swahili port, it’s a melting pot of multicultural influences.

Its the oldest living town in Kenya. There are no roads on Lamu Island, just alleyways and footpaths, and therefore, there are few motorized vehicles on the island. Residents move about on foot or by boat, and donkeys are used to transport goods and materials.
